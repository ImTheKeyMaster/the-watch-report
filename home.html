The Union League
Horology Club

At the Union League Horology Club, we celebrate a shared passion for exceptional watchmaking. Our community brings together enthusiasts — from seasoned collectors to those just beginning their journey — to enjoy and explore the art, science, and history of fine timepieces.

Discover Horological Heritage

Delve into the art, science, and history behind the world’s most celebrated timepieces. From Swiss masters to independent artisans, we shine a spotlight on the craftsmanship that makes each watch a work of art.

Learn and Share

Whether you’re a seasoned collector or just beginning your journey, our gatherings offer expert-led talks, hands‑on demonstrations, and peer-to-peer discussions that help deepen your appreciation for mechanical marvels.

Connect with Fellow Enthusiasts

Enjoy exclusive member events—workshop tours, watchmaker Q&As, and themed social evenings—in the historic halls of the Union League. Here, friendship is measured in seconds and stories are told one tick at a time.

Access Unique Opportunities

Receive priority invitations to limited-edition previews, private showings, and collaborative projects with renowned watchmakers. Our collective enthusiasm opens doors to experiences and releases not easily available elsewhere.

Join Us

If your heart beats for balance wheels and polished cases, you’ve found your circle. Join us, and let’s celebrate the precision, passion, and prestige of fine watchmaking—together.
