The Union League Horology Club

Fostering a community of timepiece connoisseurs

Mission Statement

The Union League Horology Club is dedicated to fostering a community of watch enthusiasts who appreciate the artistry, craftsmanship, and history of timepieces.

Our Purpose

To bring together watch enthusiasts to explore the art and craftsmanship of watchmaking, share their collections, and connect with like-minded members in a convivial setting.

For Members & Spouses

The club benefits Union League members and spouses by attracting and engaging members with a shared passion for fine timepieces, fostering a sense of community, and enhancing the League’s reputation as a hub for intellectual and cultural enrichment.

American Watchmaking

We cultivate a deep appreciation for the history of American watchmaking, highlighting its craftsmanship, innovation, and its vital role in our nation's history—including its contributions to the military, such as Waltham and Elgin pocket watches used during the Civil War.
